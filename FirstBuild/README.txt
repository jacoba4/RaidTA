Use the number keys (1-5) to select a unit

Use the left mouse button to tell a unit where to move or what to target


Units:
	Blue
		This is your tank, it will keep the boss occupied as long as it is hitting the boss
		
	Green
		This is your healer, it targets allied units to restore hp
		
	Red
		These are your damage dealers, they can target the boss and do big damage
		
		
		
Avoid mechanics, heal any damage you take, and defeat the boss!
